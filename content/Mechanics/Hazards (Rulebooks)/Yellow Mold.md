# Yellow Mold
#Hazard8 
#environmental #fungus 

**Stealth** DC 18 (trained)
**Description** Poisonous mold spores assault nearby creatures.
***
**Disable** DC 26 survival (expert) to remove the mold without triggering the spores
___
**Spore Explosion** `pf2:r`
**Trigger** A creature moves into the mold's space or damages the mold. The mold can't use this reaction if it's in direct sunlight or if the damage was fire damage.
**Effect** The triggering creature and all creatures within 10 feet are exposed to yellow mold spores.
___
**Yellow Mold Spores** passive
#inhaled #poison 
Any drained condition from the spores persists after the poison's duration ends.
Saving Throw DC: 26 fortitude

Maximum Duration 6 rounds
Stage 1 `dice: 1d8` poison damage and [[Drained|Drained 1]] (1 round)
Stage 2 `dice: 2d8` poison damage and [[Drained|Drained 2]] (1 round)
Stage 3 `dice: 3d8` poison damage and [[Drained|Drained 3]] (1 round)
