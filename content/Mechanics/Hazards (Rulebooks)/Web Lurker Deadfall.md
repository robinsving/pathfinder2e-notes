# Web Lurker Deadfall
#Hazard3 
#mechanical 

**Stealth** DC 13 DC 23
**Description** A web tripwire lets loose a bundle of boulders that fall on all targets within a 10-foot square.
***
**Disable** Survival (trained) or Thievery (expert) DC 20 to rearrange the webbing.
___
**Deadfall** `pf2:r`
**Trigger** A creature steps into the square with the web tripwire.
**Effect** All creatures in the trap's 10-foot square take `dice: 2d6` bludgeoning damage (DC 20 reflex save).
