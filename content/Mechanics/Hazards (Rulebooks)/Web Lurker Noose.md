# Web Lurker Noose
#Hazard2 
#mechanical #trap 

**Stealth** DC 12 
**Description** Discreet webbing at throat level snags a creature that walks into it.
***
**Disable** DC 18 survival (trained) or DC 18 thievery (expert) to rearrange the webbing.
___
**Web Noose** `pf2:r`
#attack 
**Trigger** A creature steps into the square with the web tripwire.
**Effect** The web lurker noose makes a noose Strike against the triggering creature.
___
**Noose**: `pf2:1`
Strike 13 3d6 bludgeoning 
On a successful strike, the target is [[Escape]] DC 22).
The target takes `dice: 1d6` bludgeoning damage at the end of each of its turns as long as it's caught in the noose.
