# Polymorph Trap
#Hazard12 
#magical #trap 

**Stealth** DC 24 (trained)
**Description** A Druidic glyph tries to transforms a trespasser into an animal.
***
**Disable** DC 32 thievery (master) to drain the glyph's power harmlessly, or [[Dispel Magic]] (6th rank; counteract DC 30) to counteract the glyph
___
**Cursed Metamorphosis** `pf2:r`
#primal 
**Trigger** A creature moves within 30 feet of the glyph without speaking the passphrase in Druidic
**Effect** The creature is targeted by [[Cursed Metamorphosis]] (DC: 32 will save).
