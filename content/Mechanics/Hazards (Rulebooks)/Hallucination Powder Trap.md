# Hallucination Powder Trap
#Hazard6 
#mechanical #trap 

**Stealth** DC 14 (expert)
**Description** A tube of hallucinogenic powder armed with a miniature explosive is connected to a doorknob or similar latch.
***
**Disable** DC 26 thievery (expert) to disable the hammer that strikes the percussion cap
___
**Powder Burst** `pf2:r`
#mental #poison 
**Trigger** The latch is opened or the tube is broken.
**Effect** The tube explodes, spraying hallucinogenic powder in a 30 foot cone. Any creature in the cone must succeed at a dc: 24 Will save or be confused for 1 round and take a -2 status penalty to Perception checks and saves against mental effects for `dice: 1d4` hours. On a critical failure, the penalty is instead -4.
