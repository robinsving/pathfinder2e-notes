# Steam Vents
#Hazard4 
#mechanical #trap 

**Stealth** DC 22 (trained)
**Description** Large pressurized pipes connected to a trip wire are rigged to release a blast of steam on trespassers.
***
**Disable** DC 22 thievery{Thievery (trained)} to disconnect the trip wire or a DC 24 crafting{Crafting (expert)} to carefully reduce the pressure and prevent the blast
___
**Steam Blast** `pf2:r`
**Trigger** The trip wire is pulled or severed, typically because a creature walked through the square with the trip wire
**Effect** Steam erupts from the pipes, dealing 3d6 bludgeoning damage and 3d6 fire damage (DC 24 reflex) to all creatures within 15 feet. Creatures that critically fail their save are knocked [[Prone]].
