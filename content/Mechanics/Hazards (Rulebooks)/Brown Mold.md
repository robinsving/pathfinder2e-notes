# Brown Mold
#Hazard2 
#environmental #fungus 

**Stealth** DC 11 (trained)
**Description** This unassuming fungus leeches heat out of the air.
***
**Disable** DC 18 survival (trained) to safely remove the mold
**Emit Cold** passive
#aura #cold 
5 feet [[Aura]]
Brown mold deals `dice: 2d6` cold damage to nearby creatures.
___
**Leech Warmth** `pf2:r`
**Trigger** Fire comes within 5 feet of the brown mold.
**Effect** The brown mold expands into every square adjacent to its space. As it grows, it pulls more heat from its surroundings, dealing `dice: 2d6+6` cold damage (DC 18 fortitude save) to creatures within 10 feet after it expands.
***
**Reset** After expanding, the brown mold can't grow again for 1 day.
