# Electric Latch Rune
#Hazard3 
#electricity #magical #trap 

**Stealth** DC 10 (trained)
**Description** An invisible rune imprinted on a door latch releases a powerful electric discharge.
***
**Disable** DC 20 thievery (expert) to scratch out the rune without allowing electricity to flow, or [[Dispel Magic]] (2nd rank; counteract DC 18) to counteract the rune
___
**Electrocution** `pf2:r`
#arcane #electricity 
**Trigger** A creature grasps the door latch directly or with a tool
**Effect** The trap deals `dice: 3d12` electricity damage to the triggering creature (DC 22 reflex save).
