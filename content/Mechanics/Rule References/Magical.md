# Magical

**Source** [_Core Rulebook pg. 633_](https://2e.aonprd.com/Sources.aspx?ID=1) [4.0](https://2e.aonprd.com/Sources.aspx?ID=1)  
Something with the magical trait is imbued with magical energies not tied to a specific tradition of magic. A magical item radiates a magic aura infused with its dominant school of magic.  
  
Some items or effects are closely tied to a particular tradition of magic. In these cases, the item has the [arcane](https://2e.aonprd.com/Traits.aspx?ID=11), [divine](https://2e.aonprd.com/Traits.aspx?ID=48), [occult](https://2e.aonprd.com/Traits.aspx?ID=120), or [primal](https://2e.aonprd.com/Traits.aspx?ID=134) trait instead of the magical trait. Any of these traits indicate that the item is magical.
