# Constrict `pf2:1`

**Source** [_Core Rulebook pg. 216_](https://2e.aonprd.com/Sources.aspx?ID=1) [4.0](https://2e.aonprd.com/Sources.aspx?ID=1)  
**Requirements** The snake has a smaller creature [grabbed](https://2e.aonprd.com/Conditions.aspx?ID=20).

---

The snake deals 12 bludgeoning damage to the [grabbed](https://2e.aonprd.com/Conditions.aspx?ID=20) creature; the creature must attempt a basic Fortitude save. If the snake is a specialized animal companion, increase this damage to 20.
