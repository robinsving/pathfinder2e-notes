# Magical Adaptation

When an obstacle bars your path, you pull a trick from your sleeve to overcome it. You can cast _[[Darkvision]]_, _[[Gecko Grip]]_, and 4th-level _[[Water Breathing]]_ as innate primal spells, each once per day.
